Saving a Document to Your Computer

Save as .txt

You can save your draft work to your local device, as it is always a good idea to keep copies of all of your important notes. To do so, click File from the menu bar and select Save.

Note: You can also press Ctrl + S or Command + S on your keyboard to open the Save As dialog box.

This will prompt you to enter the filename of your choice.



Once you click the Save button, it will automatically download the text file. You can find it in your Downloads folder.
