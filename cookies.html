How Online Notepad Uses Cookies

Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of text sent to your browser. It helps our website to remember information about your visit. Cookies play an important role in our web app. Without them, the text that you type in the Online Notepad editor will not be saved when you refresh the page. Please note that this app is fully client-sided so all of your data stays in your web browser. We do not store nor have the possibility to see your notes.
