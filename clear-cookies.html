How to Clear Browser Cookies

Clearing cache, cookies and browsing data will permanently delete all the notes you made within your web browser and your preferences. Make sure you save all your progress by keeping a copy of the document in your computer.

  • Chrome - Click the    button in the top right, choose History from the dropdown menu and select Clear browsing data.
  • Firefox - Press the Alt key to make the menu bar visible. From the History menu, select Clear Recent History.
  • Internet Explorer - Click the cog icon, select Internet Options and wait for the dialog box. Under Browsing History, click the Delete button.
  • Safari - Click Safari from the menubar and select Preferences from the dropdown. A new window will pop-up. Go to Privacy and click the button that says Remove All Website Data.
In most browsers, you can open the menu for deleting cookies by pressing Ctrl + Shift + Delete on Windows PC and Command + Shift + Delete for Mac.
